About EWU VCD Club

Purpose

Our club's purpose is to bring students together in a collaborative and creative environment; to perfect the art of problem solving while communicating through visual dialogues.

Goals

Strengthening student portfolios and vision, creating workshops and a visiting designer series to influence and bring in outside perspectives, and finding ways to creatively connect students with design in mind.
